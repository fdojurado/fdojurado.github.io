F. Fernando Jurado-Lasso
Postdoctoral Researcher

I am an engineer and researcher specializing in IoT, networked embedded systems, and machine learning for communications and networking. I was a postdoctoral researcher at DTU Compute, Technical University of Denmark. I hold a Ph.D. in Engineering and an M.Sc. in Telecommunications from The University of Melbourne, and a B.Sc. in Electronics Engineering from Universidad del Valle. My work focuses on advancing intelligent IoT ecosystems, with emphasis on resource management, TSCH-based networking, scheduling optimization, and autonomous adaptation using ML. I am passionate about building scalable, dependable systems that bridge embedded intelligence with real-world network performance. I also have extensive experience in teaching, mentoring, and supervising research projects.
